Egg Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Rice (as much as you like)
  • Half an Onion (as much as you like)
  • A tin of Sweetcorn
  • A few Mushrooms
  • 1-2 Eggs

Methods

  1. Start cooking the rice and put some oil in a frying pan.
  2. Chop the onion into bits and throw into the frying pan.
  3. Chop the mushrooms and add to the onions.
  4. Once the rice is cooked, drain it off and add it into the frying pan.
  5. Tip the drained sweetcorn into the pan as well and stir it all up.
  6. Break the eggs into the pan (OR beat them together in another bowl and tip into the frying pan) and stir the mixture up.
  7. Keep stirring the mixture to keep the eggs moving. Keep going until the eggs have cooked, should take about 5-8 minutes.
  8. Serve.
