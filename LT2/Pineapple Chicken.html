Pineapple Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Canola or Sesame oil
  • 1 (10 oz.) package Frozen broccoli (or stir‐fry vegetable mix), thawed*
  • ½ cup Stir-fry sauce
  • ¼ cup Pineapple juice, use reserved juice from canned pineapple
  • ¼ tsp. Garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp. crushed Red pepper (optional – use if you like it hot!)
  • 1 (15 oz.) can Pineapple chunks or tidbits, drained (reserve the juice!)
  • 2 cups Diced cooked chicken OR 2 (10 oz) cans Chicken breast, drained and flaked
*Thaw frozen vegetables in the microwave or by holding the package under cold running water for several minutes.

Methods

  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Add all ingredients EXCEPT the pineapple and chicken.
  3. Cook and stir until heated through, 5-6 minutes.
  4. Add pineapple and chicken; cook another 2 minutes.
  5. Serve over instant brown rice or whole wheat pasta. This can make 4 servings.
