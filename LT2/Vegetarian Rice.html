Vegetarian Rice

Ingredients

  • 1/2 chopped Onion
  • 1 chopped Garlic clove
  • 1 chopped Courgette
  • 1 grated Carrot
  • 1 chopped Red or yellow pepper
  • 75g frozen Peas
  • 1/2 tin of chopped Tomatoes
  • 350ml Vegetable stock
  • 150g Dried brown rice
  • 1 tbsp. of dried Parsley
  • 1 tsp. Turmeric
  • Seasoning

Methods

  1. Add onion, garlic, and turmeric to pan with a splash of vegetable stock.
  2. Stirring, cook gently for a 3 minutes, then add the chopped courgette, grated carrot, and chopped pepper.
  3. Stir well for a further 3 minutes, then add the dried rice.
  4. Stir rice in well with the vegetables.
  5. Once rice is distributed well throughout the mixture, add the stock - leave to boil.
  6. Once boiled immediately, turn heat down to simmer and cover. Cook for 10 minutes.
  7. Add in chopped tomatoes and frozen peas and stir.
  8. Cover and simmer for a further 10 minutes.
  9. Once cooked, stir in dried parsley with seasoning.
  10. Spoon rice into 2 dishes.
